TA StylesPLUGINS stands for light-weight, responsive and mobile first. All plugins are based on Tailwind CSS and Alpine JS. All are 100% customizable with endless animation options.

TA-Gallery - An image gallery with endless animation options.

TA-Pagination - An content pagination solution with endless animation options.

TA-YouTube - A YouTube video wrapper with auto playback and aspect ratio for the video player.

TA-Foodtrucks - A plugin to show foodtruck and street food dates for cities, locations and vendors.
